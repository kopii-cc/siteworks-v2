Our Services

Quality earthworks and limestone solutions for Peel homeowners and builders

🧱 Limestone Retaining Walls

Natural limestone is the material of choice for WA retaining walls — hardwearing, locally sourced, and it looks better with age. Whether you need a small garden wall or a major structural retaining wall for a sloping block, we build them to last.

Every wall is built to engineering standards: proper excavation, geotech fabric, drainage behind the stone, and compaction that holds.

  • Garden retaining walls — any height
  • Structural walls for sloping blocks
  • Curved and straight wall designs
  • Limestone steps and staircases
  • Drainage behind all walls included
  • Council compliance where required

🚧 Bulk Earthworks

Site preparation is the most important step in any build. Get it wrong and you'll be fixing problems for years. We have the gear and experience to cut, fill, grade and level your site correctly from the start.

From single residential blocks to multi-lot subdivisions, we deliver accurate cuts and fills with proper compaction.

  • Site cuts and bulk filling
  • Block levelling and grading
  • Fill importation and spreading
  • Subgrade prep for driveways and pads
  • Dam construction and repair
  • Road and access track building

💧 Drainage & Site Prep

Poor drainage destroys foundations, driveways and landscapes. We design and install practical drainage solutions that actually work in the Peel region's sandy soil conditions.

Correct site prep also means understanding your soil type, fall angles and where water needs to go. We factor all of that in.

  • Spoon drains and french drains
  • Subsoil drainage systems
  • Soakwell installation
  • Stormwater connection and outlets
  • Benching and fall correction
  • Drainage plans for council submissions
