About Us

The crew behind the limestone — Peel region, Western Australia

Our Story

Coastal Limestone started over 15 years ago as a one-man bobcat operation. One bloke, one machine, one reputation to build. That personal approach hasn't changed — we've just added more gear and more hands.

Today we handle everything from small residential retaining walls to large-scale commercial site preparation. The same care, the same quality, every time. Whether it's a garden wall in Halls Head or a multi-lot subdivision in Dawesville — we treat every job like it's our own property.

We believe in doing the job properly. That means proper drainage behind every wall, proper compaction on every pad, and leaving every site clean when we finish. That approach has earned us repeat customers and word-of-mouth referrals right across the Peel.
